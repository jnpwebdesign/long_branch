Weddings at Historic Long Branch

Nestled against the foothills of the Blue Ridge Mountains, Long Branch boasts some of the best views of the Shenandoah Valley. Our Sheila Macqueen garden is an ideal space for traditional Virginia weddings as it surrounds a beautifully groomed ceremony site. For smaller parties or inclement weather, the Main Gallery of the mansion can accommodate up to 75 guests for a more intimate Virginia wedding and reception venue. Regardless of the location you choose, Long Branch offers a Bridal Suite and Groom's Room to prepare and relax for your big day. The Bridal Suite, with views of the ceremony site, is a gorgeous upstairs room with elegant dressing room and bathroom for the bride and her bridesmaids to get ready, while the groom's “Tack Room” is an ideal spot for the groom and his groomsmen to enjoy pre-wedding preparations.
